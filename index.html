What is it ?

Created in 1997 by Jean-Jacques Rey, the 4L trophy is a humanitarian raid which initially included 3 crews, then 1500 today.This adventure is only open to students aged between 18 and 28 whose principle is to drive a Renault 4L from France to Morocco. It is a humanitarian raid with the aim of helping the education of young Moroccans. A single common word between the crews: mutual aid. To get to the end, we will have to navigate through the dunes thanks to our sense of direction.
